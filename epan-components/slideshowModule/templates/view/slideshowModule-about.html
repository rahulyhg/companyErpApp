Slide Show :

Slide show is used to display series of still images.Each image is dislayed for a fixed duration and then replaced with next image.


Installing “SlideShow” to your epan:

  • Goto market place.
  • Select “SlideShow” and click on Install button.
SlideShow will be installed to your epan and will show in “Your Applications” tab



Adding SlidShow to your epan page:

  • Goto to your epan page where you want to add slide show.
  • From toolbar Drag SlideShow and Drop where you want to add it(it can be a Container, Row or Column)
Note: if you are having any problem in dropping at desired place you may check on easy drop and show border option appeared in your toolbar.



Adding images in SlideShow:

  • Double click on SlideShow added on your page (or you can select SlideShow and press F4 key) to view “Option Window”.
  • to add images in SlideShow select Add New Image. A window will appear. This window contains images you have uploaded before or you can upload new images here.
  • To Add images select desired image and press Enter(do not use double click)

Note:

  1. If you are having any problem in dropping at desired place you may check on easy drop and show border option appeared in your toolbar.
  2. Do not select multiple images at one to add on slide show. Add them one by one.
  3. You can drag image URL in Added Slides tab to rearrange them.
  4. To remove a slide drag it to the bottom and click Delete Last Slide.
